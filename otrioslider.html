Otrio is a game based on the simple idea of tic tac toe, but taken to its next level. Players take turns on a 3 by 3 grid placing one of three different sized rings (small, medium, big) trying to either fill a row/column with their color of the same size, having the same sized ring three in a row (ala tic tac toe) or have a ascending or descending match small, medium, big in a row. If any of these are reached, that player scores an Otrio, and the game is reset.
