WDL is a 3D vehicle combat game where corporatized death and mayhem is the number 1 sport in the world. Play as one of a diverse cast of professional psychotics and utilize their tricked out rides and weapons to unleash your destructive inner child. With everyone gunning for you, can you take your place at the top of the scoreboard?
